Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

Researchers at Improbable Beef, a company that specializes in meat alternatives, have discovered that certain bacteria types are suitable for the production of tasty meat alternatives. These bacteria thrive in peoples navels. This dashboard was created to display the most common bacterial species, by count, in the navel of each volunteer test subject. If Improbable Beef is looking for people who carry a large number of a certain bacterial species, the volunteers should be able to quickly use the dashboard to figure out whether they are eligible to sell their bacteria to the company.
